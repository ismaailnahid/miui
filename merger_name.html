How it works: LEFT panel to COPY | RIGHT panel to PASTE. When ON, tapping a filename on LEFT copies its name, and tapping a filename on RIGHT replaces only its name (image remains unchanged). Perfect for theme designers to quickly merge names without altering visuals! Upload ZIP/RAR files to auto-extract images into the panels. Designed with ❤️ for MIUI Theme Studio users.
